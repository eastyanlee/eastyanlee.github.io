About Me

Dongyan (Sally) Li，现居北京。

我是首都师范大学地理信息科学专业的本科生，热衷于将AI技术与产品开发及内容运营相结合。我拥有从零构建AI驱动产品的实践经验，独立运营过多平台社交媒体账号，参与过AI辅助视频制作，并开发过数字地图平台。我擅长将复杂的技术或工具转化为易于理解的视觉或视频内容，致力于在AI运营和产品开发领域创造价值。

I am an undergraduate student in Geographic Information Science at Capital Normal University, passionate about integrating AI technology with product development and content operations. I have hands-on experience building AI-driven products from scratch, and have independently managed multi-platform social media accounts, contributed to AI-powered video production, and developed digital mapping platforms. Skilled at turning complex technologies or tools into easy-to-understand visual or video content, I am committed to creating value in AI operations and product development.
